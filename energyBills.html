Energy Bills Support

There are lots of things you can do at home to reduce the amount of energy you use and save money, from free or low-cost actions to bigger investments

Take shorter showers

Did you know that the average shower should last as long as your favourite song!

Reducing the duration of your shower can help you save £70 per year on your energy bills.


Find and fix draughts

Need an economical and more cost-effective way to save money on your energy bill?

Draught-proofing your home may be an ideal solution. A few simple steps could help plug those unwanted cold spots in your home saving you £60 per year on your energy bills.


For further information, please contact Citizen Advice


Power your home with a bicycle generator

For all you fitness fans out there that are now working from home more, why not convert that cycle-to-work scheme bike that’s just gathering dust into a bicycle generator to power your home.

Cycling 20 hours a day non stop can help you save £10 a year off your energy bills.
